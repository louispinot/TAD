Methodology

Gathering Data

We use information, data, and insights to enable you to do this job better and more efficiently. The more you know, and the more you know what matters, the higher your hourly income. You should be making around $15/hour during your first week and get to $25/hour by your 25th week.


Learning the city

Realize that as your knowledge of the city you drive in grows, so will your income. You'll drop off and pick up passengers faster, increasing your average hourly. We offer extensive material to learn your city


Using Technology

Instructions on which apps to use during your day and how they can help you. Everything from Waze to heat maps to Spotify, we'll cover everything you need to be a better driver and offer a better experience for your passengers (improving your ratings)


Surge Pricing

What should you do and where should you go? Should you drive towards the surge area? We'll tell you what you need to know about surge pricing and how to use that to your advantage.


Pool rides

The basics for more than one passenger, more than one pick up and multiple drop offs will be discussed. These are now the best rides and you will learn what to do and how to do it.


Cancelling rides

When should you cancel a passenger and what are the consequences? What if a passenger cancels while I'm on my way? These questions and much more will be answerede


Debriefing your first 25 rides

A summary of what you did right and what may have gone wrong. This is the make or break point.
