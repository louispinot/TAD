Driver Insight

Using Technology while Driving

There are many apps (FlightTracker, Waze, etc) you can learn to use that are very beneficial. For instance, did you know that if you use Waze on your phone and have a bluetooth headset, you will be notified several blocks early before you approach a traffic light intersection with a camera?


Stacking Rides

Tips on how to have a new passenger ready before you drop off the one in your vehicle? Also discussed are the dos and don'ts when using this strategy.


Strip Clubs

Knowing what to do and where to go when a passenger or group of passengers request a strip club can earn you around $10 per person if you know where to go (some clubs pay taxi drivers when they bring them customers)


Use of Multiple Apps

This section deals with the how and the why of driving for more than one app at a time. We will give you the necessary information for you to develop your own strategies.


Drop off Radius

Some apps give drivers some control over where they don't want to take passengers. Not all apps do and the ones that do don't give total control. We will cover the reasons for wanting to use this feature and how to do it.


Surge pricing

We will cover the strategies to take advantage of surge pricing and what to do when you get a suge ride.


Your App Dashboard

Understanding how to use your dashboard and getting the most out of the information it provides is key to increasing your average hourly income week after week. We will also present you other websites where useful raw data is available for you to use.
