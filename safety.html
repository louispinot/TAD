Safety

Pedestrians and Bicycles

You need to learn to develop a separate radar for anyone near or on the street who is not operating a motor vehicle. Whether they're on foot, biking or skating, they are the most vulnerable and the most unpredictable.


Pick Up & Drop Off

There is a long list of do's and don'ts when it comes to this. You can and should control passenger safety while allowing traffic to flow around you. We'll help you develop your own safe, efficient strategy.


Bluetooth & using your personal phone

You cannot do this job without a bluetooth headset. Get the best. You will use your pesonal phone for a myriad of tasks everyday. Learning to use it safely while driving is imperative.


Intersections

A 10-hour day equals 1500-2000 intersections on average. Learn what to do, what not to do, and what to watch out for.


GPS

How to use it safely? Which one to use and when should you follow or ignore it?


Control

You are the quarterback, you control the safety in avery aspect. What you do determines a successful outcome or a disaster where someone is hurt. If that happens your day is over and potentially you lose a good job.


Traffic Laws

Each city is unique in its laws and their enforcement. We will teach you what you what you need to know to safely obey the laws of your city, ultimately cutting down on fines and legal fees.
