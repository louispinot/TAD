On this website, you will find info / links to get started making money by driving your own rideshare "taxi." How much money? That is mostly up to you. Your first 100 rides (5-10 days) you may average near $20 / hour. This is after paying for gas, and the percentage that the Apps take, but does not include any money for your vehicle maintainance. As you gain insight (using the curriculum from this school) and work the learning curve, (6-12 months) your average should increase to $30 / hour. You may then find that by picking and choosing the best times to drive, you have days where you avg near $40 / hour. By all means, work long shifts when your average is high!

Frequently Asked Questions


How long will it take to get started earning money?

As little as 72 hours! What are you waiting for?

Should I drive for more than 1 App?

Absolutely. Drivers who use the most apps have the most passengers and earn the most money!

What's the very first thing to do to get started?

Sign up with the App or Apps you want to drive for at the top of this page. Then learn about the other steps it takes to become a driver here.

Why should I take a class at TAD if I'm not required to do so by any of the Apps?

There is a very real learning curve starting a brand new job. Use what we have already learned from 1,000's of rides to greatly reduce your learning curve. Let's face it, we learn from mistakes. Even after taking 1 of our courses, you will still make plenty of mistakes. The difference is you will recognize the mistake made, and then never make the same one again.

Do you Qualify?

  • 4-door vehicle from 2000 or newer
  • 21 years old or above
  • Current in-state drivers licence*
  • Current in-state auto insurance*
  • Current in-state vehicle registration
  • Mostly clean driving record
  • background check
* These documents must be in the name of the app driver
