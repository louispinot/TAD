2° Upload Documents

During the signup process, all driving apps will require the following documents:
  • Current Driver's Licence*
  • Current Auto Insurance*
  • Current Auto Registration

You can upload these pictures in the "Document" section of the App Dashboard.

You may choose to have your vehicle inspected first so you can upload all the documents, included the inspection form, in one go.

* These documents must be in the name of the app driver

3° Have your Vehicle Inspected

Take your vehicle to Jiffy Lube, Pep Boys, Goodyear, or any certified mechanic.

Lyft's vehicle inspection is done at your Mentor Ride but Uber and Sidecar require an inspection from a certified mechanic.

NB: Uber and Sidecar provide you with a specific inspection form which you will be invited to download during the signup process. You can also find them using the links on the right of this page.

Have your vehicle checked by our partner YourMechanic.com, the first mechanic who comes to you! Visit their website using the links on this page and get $20 off your first visit.

4° Download the Apps

Download all the Apps you intend to drive for onto your personal phone.

NB: Uber gives you the option ($10 / Week) to use a phone and cellular network they supply.

5° Mentor Ride

Lyft and Sidecar require new drivers to take a mentor ride.

Your mentor will inspect your vehicle to check to see that everything works and show you how to use the app. They will answer any questions you may have—we suggest you start a list.

6° Background Check

This usually takes 48 hours and you should normally be approved to start.
