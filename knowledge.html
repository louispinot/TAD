City Knowledge

How to Plan a Route

Instructions on choosing the best route for the location your passenger needs. Many factors come into play such as one-way streets, construction taking place in the area, frequently jammed streets / freeways, etc


Entering & Exiting the City

Knowing the freeway entrances and exits is a must. Your passengers will need to go to the airport, to a nearby city or many other destinations that require getting on and off the freeways.


Traffic Patterns

Good drivers pay attention to where the most traffic is and plan their routes accordingly. The better you are at avoiding traffic, the more money you will make.


Bridges, Airports, Buses and Trains

Even though the destination is entered for you on the App and each App has GPS, it pays to know the locations of places your passengers repeatedly request.


The Grid

Learn ways to quickly master the order of street names. Short cuts and tricks you can use to navigate the grid in days instead of months!


Scientific Approach

Rather than just driving around and trying to memorize streets names, this section will explain a logical process you can use to cut months off the learning curve
